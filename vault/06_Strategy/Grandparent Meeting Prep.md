# Grandparent Company Meeting Preparation

## Objective
Present findings to ATI's parent company to secure:
1. **Investment capital** for Apollo (top ask)
2. **Independent financial controls** — Apollo needs direct access to its own accounts
3. **Audit trail implementation** on the accounting system
4. **Independent review** of ATI's management of Apollo's finances

## Framing Strategy
- **Frame as:** Misallocation and control failures, not theft
- **Tone:** Professional, evidence-driven, concerned
- **Avoid:** Direct accusations that trigger defensive responses from Jimmy/Rosanna
- **Acknowledge:** Where reasonable alternative explanations exist — this builds credibility

## Presentation Structure
See [[Evidence Tiers]] for the tiered approach.

## Key Slides / Talking Points
1. Apollo's financial structure (ATI controls everything, no audit trail)
2. The fuel cost gap ($287K, independently verified)
3. The pattern (all anomalies move in one direction)
4. Specific manipulation evidence (backdating, batch-printing, mass deletion)
5. What we're asking for (capital + controls)

## Risk Assessment
- Jimmy and Rosanna will deny and deflect — prepare for this
- They may claim clerical errors — the directional consistency argument defeats this
- They may attack Walker's credibility — independent verification (IFTA, bank) is the shield
- The grandparent may not want to act — the financial exposure argument is the lever
