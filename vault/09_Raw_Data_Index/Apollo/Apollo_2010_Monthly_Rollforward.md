## Contents Summary
**KEY EVIDENCE FILE.** Monthly rollforward schedule for Apollo Energy GL Account 2010 (Fuel Payable / Intercompany Payable). Shows opening balance, monthly debits/credits, and closing balance for each month across the investigation period. This file is the primary proof that Account 2010 grew systematically month-over-month throughout 2024 as ATI posted recurring fuel cost charges. The rollforward demonstrates the ratchet pattern: the balance only increased during 2024, with no offsetting payments or adjustments until 2025 clearing entries. Critical for establishing the timeline and magnitude of the fuel payable accumulation scheme.
