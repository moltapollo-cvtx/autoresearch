**KEY EVIDENCE FILE.** Detailed transaction listing for Apollo Energy GL Account 2010 (Fuel Payable / Intercompany Payable) through 2025. Account 2010 is the central mechanism through which ATI accumulated fraudulent fuel-related charges against Apollo. In 2025, clearing entries were posted that attempted to settle the inflated balance through intercompany transfers and chargebacks.
