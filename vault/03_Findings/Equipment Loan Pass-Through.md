# Equipment Loan Pass-Through — ATI-Titled Loans Charged to Apollo

## Summary

ATI's own chargeback document ([[WHAT ATI CLAIMS APOLLO OWES FROM 2025 CHARGEBACKS]]) reveals that **$1,655,968** in equipment loans across 9 units are being charged to Apollo at **$36,863/month ($442,358/year)**. Of these, at least 3 units ($785,695 in principal) are titled to **"Asphalt"** (ATI), not Apollo. If Apollo is paying loan installments on equipment ATI owns, this is a direct transfer mechanism.
